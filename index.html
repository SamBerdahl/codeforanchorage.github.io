Code for Anchorage

Our Repositories.

We are working on trail, transit, and texting apps and local implementations of open source projects. Have an idea for a project? Stop by one of our bi-weekly hack nights and recruit help.

Current and past projects

  • Balance - Check your SNAP (Alaska Quest Card, Food Stamp) Balance via texting.
  • Balance (Spanish) - Check your SNAP (Alaska Quest Card, Food Stamp) Balance via texting. We are looking for help translating Balance into other languages if you would like to help.
  • Find Yourself in Kincaid Park is an app by Brigade member, Mike Brook, that uses Code for Anchorage Trail data from the Muni, DNR and Single Track Advocates.
  • Click that Hood is a web game for learning neighborhoods and geography. Brigade members have added data for the following:
    • Anchorage Neighborhoods
    • Anchorage Neighborhoods
    • Indigenous Peoples and Languages of Alaska
    • Alaska Iditarod Trail (Even Years)
    • Canada (Provinces) Start here to build confidence.
